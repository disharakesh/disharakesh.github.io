Me, in a nutshell 🥜

Hi, I'm Disha — part analyst, part professional question-asker. I light up when a tangled mess of numbers finally starts to make sense, and I'm quietly incapable of leaving a clunky process alone.

I came into analytics from computer engineering, did my MSc in Business Analytics at UCC, and now I spend my days turning messy data into things people can actually act on — forecasts, dashboards, cleaner ways of working. I care less about fancy models and more about whether the answer genuinely helps someone make a better call.

I'm reliable, deeply curious, and somehow the one who remembers everyone's random stories from years ago. I think the best ideas tend to start as wonderfully chaotic conversations — so if you've got a knot in your data, I'd love to help untangle it.
